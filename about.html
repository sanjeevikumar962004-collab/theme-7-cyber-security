Our Mission

Engineering Trust.

We exist to protect the digital frontier. Stackly was founded on the principle that absolute security shouldn't compromise speed, innovation, or scale.

Origins

Born from the breach.

In 2018, witnessing the cascading failures of legacy firewalls against sophisticated state-sponsored attacks, a group of offensive security researchers and systems architects came together.

We realized that defending modern infrastructure required a fundamental shift: moving away from perimeter-based defense to a mathematically rigorous, zero-trust paradigm. Today, Stackly secures over $500B in daily enterprise transactions.

Core Values

The Stackly Protocol.

Zero Assumptions

We trust nothing by default. Every packet, every identity, and every request must mathematically prove its legitimacy before execution.

Offensive Defense

To build impenetrable walls, you must think like the attacker. Our defensive strategies are continuously tested by elite red teams.

Radical Transparency

Security through obscurity is a myth. We open-source our core protocols and subject our infrastructure to continuous public audits.
